Reminder: Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping," numbers divisible by 5 become "pong," numbers divisible by 15 become "ping-pong."

How to Play:

Ping Pong will count up to your number starting with 1 with the following exceptions

  • Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong"
  • Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping-pong"
